¡how to get temproot by 'getroot'

usageF
push 'getroot' in device, set permission, and execute 'getroot'

> adb push getroot /data/local/tmp
> adb shell
$ cd /data/local/tmp
$ chmod 0755 getroot
$ ./getroot
